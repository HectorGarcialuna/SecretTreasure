Here's a riddle for you:

"I am a dance in your mouth, a party on your plate.
A melody from Spain, in Mexico, a mate.
When the moon is high, I am a favorite sight.
Wrapped in a blanket, under the starlight.

I come in many forms, with spices or plain.
In the morning or at night, your hunger I tame.
Pair me with something soft, and round as the moon,
Bite after bite, you'll be humming a tune.

What am I?"
